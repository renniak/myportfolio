{{About Me}}

Let me tell you a few things...

When I was 14 years old, unaware of what I was doing, I gave my first baby steps in web development; playing with Dreamweaver and Macromedia Flash I ended up making a website for my high school, since then I have always been fascinated with the internet and technology.

After years of studying and working in the Biology field and by mere serendipity I got a job in the IT field and rediscovered my passion for web development. As a hobby and a personal challenge I started teaching myself new technologies as well as programming. I've decided that I want to pursue a career in this field.

It might seem that biology and web development have nothing in common but in my opinion it all comes down to solving problems. And to me that's one of the best feelings in the entire world: leaving a solution where a problem used to be.
